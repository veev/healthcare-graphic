Who is Covered and How

By all insurance plans

Seniors
Cannot be charged more than 5X as much as a young person

Women
Cannot be charged more than men for the same plan

Young People
Under 26 years old can stay on their parents' plan

People with pre-existing conditions
Cannot be charged more for the same plan

Sick people
Cannot be charged more than healthy people for the same plan

Under ACA
States must require these health insurance protections

Under AHCA
States would be allowed to scale back these protections completely. (via waver request)
